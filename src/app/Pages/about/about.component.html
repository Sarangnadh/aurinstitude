About Us

AURA Institute of Interior Design & Fashion Technology

AURA Institute of Interior Design & Fashion Technology is a premier educational institute that fosters creative innovation and technical excellence. Our mission is to nurture budding designers and equip them with the knowledge and skills to thrive in the dynamic fields of interior design and fashion technology.

Established with a vision to create industry-ready professionals, AURA offers a range of programs accredited by renowned universities, with an emphasis on hands-on learning, industry exposure, and an integrated approach to both traditional and modern design.

Our Mission

To empower students with innovative design education, helping them become future leaders in the interior and fashion industries.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in design education, setting the benchmark for creativity, ethics, and professionalism.

Our Values

Creativity, Integrity, Innovation, and Commitment to Excellence in every endeavor.
